About Us

Welcome to the home page for the Valiant Phoenix competetive group. We are a growing group focused around making fun and cooperativly functional teams. Our aim is to create the best competitive experience for everyone, and toxicity will not be tolerated. Anyone is welcome to join, provided they meet our basic requirements, and tryouts are held for all applicants. This is to ensure that all new members sync well with our existing members. Tryouts are held at specific times agreed upon by all participants. Please refer to the official list of rules to view all of our requirements.
